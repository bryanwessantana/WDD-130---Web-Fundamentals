Purpose

Our company created this website so our clients and students can get a better access to their agenda, contents, contacts, activities (audios, texts, images) and support from the teachers to help them.

Audience

We are always looking for clients that need help to learn a new language. We have technical English, for travel, work, study, exams (preparing them for TOEFL and IELTS). And as different English School, we also work with language consulting, doing translations (academic, literary, websites, etc).

Content

Home page

People can find answers on our website when looking for courses and materials we have, so they can use and receive the education and help they need. They can find video tutorials, books, activities, audio, films, and history books to improve their English. If they have some doubts that are not on our website, they can contact us to schedule a meeting and talk to one of our employees and teachers. If they already were clients and students, they can find out when the meetings will happen, how their assignments and grades are going, and if there is something they need to send or correct in their activities with our feedback and rubric system.

Images for the Home page

English Standard & Educational Games

They can see how their English is with our English Standard Test, and then if they want something more specific, they can save their login on our site to talk to us and schedule a meeting. Also, they can do some easy activities like listening or reading something and complete activities to receive points and learn more. They can use these points to receive gifts from the school. Also, if they accumulate specific punctuation on the website, they can apply it at a discount on their monthly payment.

Images for the Page 2

Applying English at Different Areas

They will come to our website because we will respond and contact them always with priority and care to fulfill their requests. We can make them grow their English with efficiency and quality so they will always prioritize us to care about them and teach them in the best way possible. We already have clients and students that have been with us for a few years, and they all were happy and reached their goals of traveling to many countries and applying their new abilities and skills in their jobs and universities. Many of them have done the TOEFL and IELTS and have passed.
